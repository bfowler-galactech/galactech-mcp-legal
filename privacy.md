# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 16, 2026

Galactech Consulting Group LLC ("Galactech", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our software.

## Information We Collect

This software may access data from your connected QuickBooks Online account solely to perform requested operations. We do not collect, store, or transmit your QuickBooks data to any third party.

## How We Use Information

Information accessed via the software is used only to execute the operations you initiate. No data is retained outside the runtime session unless explicitly saved by you.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

## Security

We follow reasonable measures to protect any information handled by the software, but no system is completely secure.

## Your Rights

You may revoke the software's access to your QuickBooks account at any time via your Intuit account settings.
